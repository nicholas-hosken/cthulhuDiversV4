Included on your Chichén Itzá tour:

  • Beat the crowds and midday heat on an early-access tour of Chichen Itza.
  • Gain priority access to the wonder of the world and explore in more comfortable temperatures.
  • An archeologist guide provides important historical and cultural details
  • A boxed lunch and round-trip transfers from meeting points are included.

Your tour begins with an early morning departure. Arriving at Chichen Itza, join your specialized guide for an in-depth tour of the site, and enjoy the tranquility of your early-morning visit before the crowds arrive. Throughout the trip, your archaeological guide's commentary provides essential background to the well-preserved site of Chichen Itza. Learn how this large pre-Colombian city played an important role in Mayan civilization from around A.D. 600 to 1200, and remained a center of worship and pilgrimage until the arrival of the Spanish. Even today, it is still considered a sacred site by the Yucatán peninsula's Maya population. Like many of ancient Mexico's cities, the site was abandoned, and its ruins were hidden by jungle. Hear how the site was discovered and unearthed by archaeologists, gaining UNESCO protection and considered today one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. Your guide will explain the philosophy, cosmology and mythology behind this ancient site, such as the meaning of the stone carvings, pyramids, platforms, ball courts and staircases. Visit the Observatory, Temple of the Jaguars, Temple of the Warriors and of course the famous El Castillo, also known as the Temple of Kukulkan.

Chichén Itzá Important Info

  • It is recommended that you bring comfortable walking shoes and camera.
  • Note: The tour is on shared basis and there is a private guide per each group of up to 20 people.
  • Please note that all visitors to Chichen Itza will need to pay a local tax of $417 mxn. Only credit card payment the day ot the tour.
  • Not wheelchair accessible.
  • Stroller accessible.
  • Infants must sit on laps.
  • We care about your safety and we guarantee a safe route by toll road to Chichen Itza.
  • This tour is special needs friendly. Please contact Cthulhu Divers with any concerns or requests.
  • This tour/activity will have a maximum of 60 travelers.
